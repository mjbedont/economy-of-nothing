Backstory

The Moonshine began as an ordinary red pickup truck on Planet Prime. After Captain Raymond B. Tracy bought it for simple hauls, an unforeseen hyperspace anomaly transformed it into an interstellar marvel. This humble truck became the iconic vessel for the Moonshine Crew, evolving into a hybrid of rugged Earth design and advanced space tech.

Key Moments

  • Transported the Brettson Rangers to the site of the Skull Pirate Ambush
  • Survived a critical hit during the Hyperspace Paradox
  • Outmaneuvered Technobandit fighter crafts in the Dark Orbit
