Summary

  • Personality: Bold, loud, idealistic
  • Affiliations: Nothing Corporation, Moonshine Crew
  • Episode Appearances: All episodes

Biography

Alex Brettson is the older of Captain Tracy’s two nephews—and he takes his role as a Junior Ranger very seriously. Brave to a fault, Alex believes space needs heroes, and he’s willing to jump headfirst into danger to prove he’s one. He’s memorized every rule in the Ranger Manual but often forgets to ask if they apply. Loyal to his uncle and fiercely protective of his younger brother Erik, Alex is convinced the best way to learn is by doing. And shouting.

Key Moments

  • Volunteered for duty on the Moonshine Insisted on a full Ranger ceremony. Captain Tracy handed him a flashlight and saluted.
  • Saved a village from Skull Pirates Mostly by yelling. Still counts.
  • Challenged Dispatch security to a duel It was not a duel. He was tased. But they agreed to re-review his Ranger credentials.

Role in the Universe

Alex represents raw enthusiasm. He believes in the promise of the system even when others have lost faith. His courage often puts him in danger, but his idealism reminds the rest of the crew why they fight in the first place. If the universe is going to be saved, Alex wants to be the one holding the flag when it happens.
