Captain Tracy, You're the Problem: Protagonist Privilege and Birthday Chaos

Is the Captain the hero—or just the loudest person on the crew? In this birthday special, Mike, Ryan, and Mike Allen break down Captain Tracy’s ego, Cheyenne’s competence, and Mr. Green’s manipulative genius. One of them also has a birthday. It does not go well.
