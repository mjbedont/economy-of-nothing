Cowboys vs. Pirates: A New Idea

Dispatch 001: Captain Tracy leads the Moonshine Crew to the dusty corporate wasteland of Brenner 7 for their first real dispatch job. What starts as a simple freight run turns into a standoff with the Skull Pirates, a confrontation with Marshal Robert, and a deeply suspicious invoice.

The episode kicks off the series with exactly the right kind of chaos: broken systems, stubborn idealists, and a crew trying to carve out a path in a rigged galaxy. Also, a surprise alliance with freelancers and a robot sheriff. The pirates might not even be the worst part.

🧾 Notable Moments

  • 🚀 Tracy lands on Brenner 7 (after two failed attempts)
  • 🤖 Introduction of Marshal Robert
  • 💥 Town standoff with Skull Pirates and freelancers
  • 📦 Missing cargo turns out to be literally “Nothing”
  • 📡 Mr. Green says: “Trust the paperwork, not the people.”

📚 Lore References

  • Brenner 7 – Former entertainment hub turned mining colony
  • Planet Prime – Bureaucratic capital of the Nothing Corporation

  • The Badlands – Rocky terrain surrounding the Skull Pirate camp
  • Skull Pirates – Ruthless raiders with unclear motives
  • Marshal Robert – The last lawman of Brenner 7
  • Zella – Sole surviving townsperson of Brenner 7
