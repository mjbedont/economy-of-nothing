Biography

Erik Brettson is the youngest member of the Moonshine crew—and the most technologically gifted. Where Alex rushes into danger, Erik hangs back, scanning every variable. He rebuilt half the Moonshine’s comm systems using scrap and instinct. Though his quiet brilliance makes him invaluable, he struggles with insecurity, especially when compared to his older brother. He’s not sure if he’s meant for heroism... but he’s trying anyway.

Role in the Universe

Erik is the crew’s quiet conscience and soft-hearted realist. Where Alex believes in heroes, Erik believes in systems—and wants to know why they always break. He represents the tension between idealism and pragmatism, and his growth from hesitant passenger to capable leader may shape the next generation more than anyone realizes.
