Backstory

Brenner 7 is a rugged mining planet located in the Outer Corporate Territories. It blends the aesthetics of a classic frontier world with the harsh realities of corporate control. Rich in natural resources, it serves as a critical supplier for the Nothing Corporation’s hyperspace operations.

The planet’s inhabitants, while resilient and self-reliant, are caught in a constant struggle between their desire for freedom and the economic grip of their corporate overlords. The harsh desert environment is home to rogue AI remnants and other dangers, making survival a daily challenge.

Key Moments

  • The Moonshine Crew’s first mission took place on Brenner 7, where they confronted the Skull Pirates.
  • Major mining strikes on Brenner 7 led to the first major rebellion against the Nothing Corporation.
  • Rogue AI units, known as Rabid Cowboys, have been a persistent threat to the planet’s settlers.

Role in the Universe

Brenner 7 represents the extreme dichotomy of freedom and control that defines the galaxy in The Economy of Nothing. Its importance as a resource hub makes it a valuable asset to the Nothing Corporation, while its frontier spirit serves as an inspiration to those resisting corporate domination. The planet’s conflicts and struggles have a ripple effect across the Outer Corporate Territories.
