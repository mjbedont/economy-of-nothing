Biography

Mr. Green is a member of an enigmatic alien race, shrouded in secrecy. His true origins remain largely unknown, and his history is a complex web of hidden motives, diplomatic missions, and behind-the-scenes influence. He first appeared as a mysterious figure within the ranks of the Dispatch, offering his skills as a diplomat and tactician to Captain Tracy and his crew. His advanced intellect and cryptic nature make him an invaluable, albeit unpredictable, ally.

Key Moments

  • Brokered peace between the Technobandits and the Skull Pirates
  • Facilitated the Hyperspace Paradox event, changing the fabric of reality
  • Unveiled his true motives in a high-stakes mission during the Dark Orbit
  • Assisted in the formation of the Dispatch as a strategic advisor
