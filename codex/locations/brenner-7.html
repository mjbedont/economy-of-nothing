Brenner 7

  • Region: Outer Corporate Territories
  • Controlled By: The Nothing Corporation
  • Primary Industry: Mining and Resource Extraction
  • Notable Events: Moonshine Crew's First Mission
  • Key Characters: Captain Tracy, Mr. Green, Skull Pirates

Brenner 7 is a corporate-run cowboy planet, blending frontier aesthetics with the oppressive oversight of The Nothing Corporation. A rugged mining planet in the Outer Corporate Territories, it represents the extreme dichotomy of freedom and control in The Economy of Nothing.

Governed with an iron fist, Brenner 7's inhabitants are caught between their desire for independence and the economic stranglehold of the corporation. Resources are stripped at an alarming rate, and dissent is met with swift and often brutal suppression.
