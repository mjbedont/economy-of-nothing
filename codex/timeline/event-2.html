Summary

  • Key Figures: High Chancellor Niven, Fleet Admiral Carroway

Event Description

The Foundation of the Confederacy was a pivotal moment in humanity's expansion into the stars. In response to increasing conflicts among colonies, representatives from key factions convened to form an alliance. The Confederacy established the first interstellar government, regulating hyperspace travel and maintaining order across newly colonized planets.

Key Moments

  • Signing of the Confederate Charter
  • Creation of the Confederate Navy
  • Establishment of the Universal Colonization Code
  • Election of High Chancellor Niven as the first leader

Role in the Universe

The Confederacy laid the groundwork for interstellar diplomacy, setting precedents that would shape galactic politics for centuries. Despite its eventual decline, its legacy persists in modern galactic governance and corporate treaties.
