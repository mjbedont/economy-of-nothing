Earth 20XX

  • Region: Sol System
  • Known For: Humanity’s Last Peaceful Era
  • Notable Events: Captain Tracy’s Departure
  • Key Characters: Captain Tracy

Earth 20XX marks the final chapter of humanity’s quiet existence before being thrust into a future dominated by hyperspace travel and corporate greed. This was Captain Tracy’s home, a simpler time that he left behind when hyperspace paradoxes drew him into the distant future.

While Earth 20XX may appear ordinary, it represents the humanity and simplicity that Tracy fights to protect in the fractured cosmos of The Economy of Nothing.
