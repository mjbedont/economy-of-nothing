The Beginning of Humanity's Leap

Summary

  • Event: Hyperspace Exodus
  • Year: Unknown (pre-Captain Tracy Era)
  • Significance: Humanity’s first step into the stars
  • Outcome: Initiated interstellar colonization
  • Key Locations: Planet Prime, New Rome, Dracronaria

Event Description

The Hyperspace Exodus marked a turning point for humanity, enabling interstellar travel through the newly discovered hyperspace corridors. This event set the stage for the fractured universe we know today, as colonies expanded and corporations began to rise to power.

Key Moments

  • Discovery of hyperspace travel
  • Colonization of planets such as Planet Prime and New Rome
  • Emergence of corporate dominance in interstellar governance
  • Formation of early factions, including the precursors to the Skull Pirates

Role in the Universe

The Hyperspace Exodus reshaped humanity’s destiny, paving the way for the fractured galaxy that serves as the backdrop for the modern conflicts of the Dispatch Era.
