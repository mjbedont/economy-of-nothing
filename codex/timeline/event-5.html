A New Beginning

Summary

  • Event: Captain Tracy Leaves Retirement
  • Year: 5 Years After the Robo-Human War
  • Significance: Tracy’s return to active service with the Nothing Company
  • Outcome: Tracy becomes captain of the Moonshine Crew
  • Key Figures: Captain Raymond B. Tracy, Alex Brettson, Erik Brettson

Event Description

After the end of the Robo-Human War, Captain Tracy retired to a quiet life, seeking peace after years of galactic conflict. However, five years later, his two nephews, Alex and Erik Brettson, arrived with news of opportunities at the Nothing Company. Their enthusiasm and Tracy’s lingering sense of duty inspired him to leave retirement and accept a position as captain of the Moonshine Crew.

His leadership quickly became instrumental in managing the chaotic landscape of corporate logistics and interstellar adventures, shaping the fate of the galaxy once again.

Key Moments

  • Arrival of Alex and Erik Brettson at Tracy’s retirement home
  • Decision to join the Nothing Company
  • Formation of the Moonshine Crew
  • First deployment to Dispatch 69 on Planet Prime

Role in the Universe

Tracy’s return to active service marked the beginning of a new era. His leadership and unconventional tactics revitalized the Nothing Company’s operations and positioned the Moonshine Crew as a critical force in interstellar politics, commerce, and adventure.
