Backstory

Earth 20XX represents the final chapter of humanity’s quiet existence before being thrust into a future dominated by hyperspace travel and corporate greed. This was Captain Tracy’s home, a world of simplicity, stability, and limited technological advancement compared to the fractured cosmos he would later navigate.

Although mundane by interstellar standards, Earth 20XX is a symbol of what humanity lost in its pursuit of the stars—a peaceful world free of hyperspace paradoxes, corporate wars, and existential threats.

Key Moments

  • Captain Tracy’s unexpected departure, marking the end of Earth’s influence in galactic events.
  • The first experimental hyperspace theories developed on Earth.
  • The beginning of Earth’s decline as a central power in the galaxy.

Role in the Universe

Earth 20XX serves as a reminder of humanity’s origins and the values lost in the transition to a fractured galaxy. It is a symbol of hope and nostalgia for Captain Tracy, driving his mission to preserve what remains of humanity's essence in the chaos of The Economy of Nothing.
