"The Pirate Captain"

Summary

  • Role: Pirate Captain, Leader of the Skull Pirates
  • Personality: Ruthless, cunning, pragmatic idealist
  • Affiliations: Skull Pirates
  • Episode Appearances: Episode 1, others TBD

Biography

Once a respected tactician in the outer systems, Washington abandoned the corporate chain of command and forged his own pirate empire. Disillusioned with the Nothing Corporation's endless cycle of debt and exploitation, he took matters into his own hands—by turning outlaw.

Key Moments

  • First appeared during the Battle of Brenner 7 Orchestrated a large-scale mining takeover and forced labor operation at the edge of Nothing Corp territory.
  • Faced off against Captain Tracy under false pretenses Mistook a rescue mission for corporate sabotage; parleyed with Tracy before hostilities escalated.

Role in the Universe

Washington represents the brutal alternative to corporate control—a rebel with his own code who sees the hypocrisy of the system, yet replicates its violence in pursuit of freedom. His presence forces the viewer to ask: is freedom still freedom when it comes at the cost of others?
