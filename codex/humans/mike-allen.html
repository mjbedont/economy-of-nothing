Biography

Mike Allen co-hosts The Economy of Nothing Podcast, lending a grounded perspective and dry wit to a show otherwise adrift in cosmic nonsense. He is credited with coining the term "Business Mike" and was the first to receive the Regifted Genius Star for accidental brilliance.
