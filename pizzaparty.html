Welcome to Pizza Party

Dive into the chaos with our collection of games set in the world of The Economy of Nothing. Play, explore, and conquer!

How to Play Cosmic Map:

  • Start your journey on Planet Prime, where you can purchase fuel or promotions.
  • Navigate to other planets by clicking on them in the galactic map. Each trip consumes fuel based on the distance traveled.
  • Complete missions on planets to earn credits. Missions can only be completed once per planet before returning to Planet Prime.
  • Buy fuel at local prices or promotions (Planet Prime only) to improve your dispatch statistics.
  • Your POD (Promotions per Dispatch) is tracked. It's calculated as the number of promotions divided by the number of dispatches. Aim for the highest POD!
  • The game ends if you run out of fuel while traveling. Your final score is determined by your POD, with credits serving as a tiebreaker.

Good luck, captain! May your POD be ever high.
