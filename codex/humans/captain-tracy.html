Biography

Subject Raymond B. Tracy was once an ordinary individual from Earth in the year 20XX. His life changed drastically when he was drafted into the Robo-Human Wars. Although initially reluctant to take on such a monumental role, Tracy soon became the captain of the Moonshine, leading the crew through numerous challenges. Known for his pragmatic idealism and unwavering moral compass, Tracy became a symbol of hope and resistance in the universe. As a leader, he navigated the complexities of intergalactic diplomacy while dealing with the personal struggles of his crew.

Key Moments

  • Made peace with the Technobandits to end a long-standing war
  • Defended the Nothing Corporation from corporate sabotage
  • Uncovered the Hyperspace Paradox that reshaped the universe's timeline

Role in the Universe

As Captain of the Moonshine, Tracy's leadership played a pivotal role in the formation of the Dispatch. He is both a hero and a reluctant leader, guiding his crew through the many twists and turns of the fragmented universe.
