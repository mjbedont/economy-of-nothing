Biography

Ranger Erik Brettson, the 9-year-old nephew of Captain Tracy, is a brilliant tech expert and a junior ranger with the Moonshine Crew. Despite his young age, Erik is a whiz with technology and can often be found tinkering with gadgets or assisting the crew with repairs and upgrades. His thoughtful nature and keen intelligence make him an invaluable asset, though his insecurity sometimes holds him back from fully embracing his potential.

Erik deeply admires his older brother, Alex, and Captain Tracy, but he struggles with his own sense of self-worth. Despite his reservations, Erik's contributions to the team have proven to be essential, and his technical expertise has saved the crew on more than one occasion. His journey is one of self-discovery and growth, learning to overcome his insecurities and realize his true capabilities.

Key Moments

  • Developed the communications device that saved the crew during the Skull Pirate skirmish
  • Repaired the Moonshine after it was severely damaged in the Battle of Brenner 7
  • Created a hacking tool that helped the crew infiltrate Technobandit communications
  • Assisted in the Hyperspace Paradox event with his quick-thinking and tech skills

Role in the Universe

As a junior tech expert with a growing sense of confidence, Erik’s role within the Moonshine Crew is pivotal. Though he may be young, his intellect and skill set make him a key player when it comes to solving technical problems and ensuring the crew's survival in hostile situations. Erik’s journey is one of overcoming self-doubt and finding his place among the stars, and with the mentorship of Captain Tracy and his family, he is destined to grow into a leader of his own.
