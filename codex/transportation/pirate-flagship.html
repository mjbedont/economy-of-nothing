"The Black Nebula"

Summary

  • Type: Space Pirate Flagship
  • Role: Command Ship
  • Affiliations: Skull Pirates
  • Key Features: Broadside Cannons, Boarding Hooks
  • Episode Appearances: Episodes 6, 10, 14

Backstory

Known across the galaxy as the Black Nebula, this terrifying vessel is the pride of the Skull Pirates. Its design pays homage to old Earth pirate ships, complete with sails (for aesthetic purposes) and a hull carved to resemble a grinning skull. Armed to the teeth and crewed by the galaxy’s most fearsome bandits, the Black Nebula is both a nightmare and a legend.

Key Moments

  • Destroyed a Dispatch fleet in Episode 6
  • Orchestrated the Space Corsair Assault in Episode 10

Specifications

  • Length: 500 meters
  • Propulsion: Dark Matter Engines
  • Defenses: Plasma Shields
  • Offenses: Energy Broadsides, Gravity Cannons
  • Unique Features: Pirate aesthetics, hidden escape pods

Role in the Universe

The Black Nebula serves as the central command for the Skull Pirates, embodying their chaotic and fearsome nature. With its larger-than-life appearance and devastating firepower, it’s a recurring threat in the series, constantly testing the ingenuity and courage of the Moonshine Crew.
