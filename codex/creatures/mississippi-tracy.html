Biography

Mississippi, a former Earth dog in the 20XX era, became a highly intelligent being after a series of future space phenomena that enhanced her cognitive abilities. Originally Captain Raymond B. Tracy’s dog, Mississippi's life took a dramatic turn when her intelligence was amplified by mysterious forces linked to advanced space technology.

After the enhancement, Mississippi gained the ability to communicate with humans and other species, though her primary mode of communication remains through barks, gestures, and occasional clever use of technology. She now works at the Nothing Corporation, serving as a highly valuable consultant for the company’s research and development division, with a particular focus on animal behavior studies and inter-species communication.

Her time with Captain Tracy in the 20XX era remains a fond memory for both her and Tracy, though they have both moved on to different roles in the future. Mississippi’s loyalty and sharp intellect make her a respected figure, even among the other more traditional employees at the Nothing Corporation.

Key Moments

  • Survived the Space Warp that triggered her cognitive enhancement
  • Helped Captain Tracy navigate the Skull Pirates in Episode 1
  • Played a pivotal role in the Nothing Corporation's experiments with inter-species communication in Episode 4
  • Led a rescue operation during the Hyperspace Paradox, saving Tracy’s crew in Episode 6

Role in the Universe

As an intelligent dog, Mississippi bridges the gap between animals and humans in the Codex of Nothing universe. Though still a dog at heart, her enhanced intelligence makes her an essential asset to the Nothing Corporation and a source of great wisdom for Captain Tracy. Mississippi’s deep connection with Tracy, combined with her newfound abilities, gives her a unique place in the universe—both as a companion and as a scientific marvel. Whether helping with technical experiments or simply offering emotional support to the crew, Mississippi continues to prove that even the simplest creatures can have an extraordinary impact on the universe.
