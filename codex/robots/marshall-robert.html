Biography

Marshall Robert is a highly advanced combat robot created by the **Nothing Corporation** to serve as a protector and guardian. Initially built to assist in maintaining order within the **Dispatch**, he quickly gained a reputation for his unwavering loyalty and formidable combat skills. Marshall Robert is equipped with advanced AI that allows him to make complex decisions in the heat of battle, but his core programming still centers around protecting the people he is sworn to defend.

Key Moments

  • Saved **Captain Tracy** and his crew during an ambush by the **Skull Pirates** in Episode 3
  • Assisted in the rescue of the **Moonshine Crew** from the **Technobandits** in Episode 6
  • Neutralized a rogue robot uprising within the **Nothing Corporation**

Role in the Universe

Marshall Robert plays a critical role as the primary protector of the **Dispatch** and its key members. His high-level AI allows him to function not only as a combat expert but also as a tactical advisor in critical situations.
