Mr. Green's Laser Pointer

🔴 Tactical Distraction Tool. Also Cat Toy.

Codex Home 🛸 Mr. Green

Overview

Originally issued as part of a NothingCorp presentation kit, this laser pointer found new life as Mr. Green’s favorite “non-lethal” problem solver. It's been used to disorient foes, distract bureaucrats, and once, to herd a litter of cloned space-cats.

Known Uses

  • Distraction: Especially effective on interns and surveillance orbs
  • Signaling: Works great if you're standing in the wrong place at the right time
  • Weapon Fusion: Temporarily fused with a whip, creating an “energy whip” Cheyenne refuses to acknowledge
