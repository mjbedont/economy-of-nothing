Mr. Green's Whip

🤠 One Leather Whip. No Refunds. Infinite Problems.

Codex Home 🛸 Mr. Green 🤠 Brenner 7

Overview

Mr. Green picked up this completely normal leather bullwhip during a mission on Brenner 7. The vendor claimed it had “historical authenticity.” Mr. Green just liked the sound it made.

Specifications

  • Material: Leather, age unknown
  • Grip: Worn, possibly cursed
  • Range: Just far enough to be a nuisance

Usage Notes

The whip is not magical, weaponized, or enhanced in any way. That hasn’t stopped Mr. Green from using it like it is. Whether cracking cans, wrangling orphans, or disarming smugglers, he insists it's "the most versatile thing in the galaxy."
