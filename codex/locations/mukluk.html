Overview

Mukluk, once known (and rightfully mocked) as Snow Boots, is a frozen resort world rebranded by the Nothing Corporation’s Lifestyle Acquisition Division. Beneath the glossy chalet brochures and hand-curated snowfall lies a darker truth: it’s a favorite drop zone for freelance bounty hunters and corporate debt enforcers.

Tourism Highlights

  • Notable Terrain: Endless powder fields, hidden crevasses, and suspiciously well-heated lodges
  • Peak Activity: Apres-ski shootouts, sauna ambushes, polite interrogation in hot tubs
  • Key Exports: Designer cold-weather gear, memories people paid to forget, missing persons reports

In the Show

Mukluk serves as the icy pressure cooker for a psychological cat-and-mouse arc between Captain Tracy and Erik. Stranded during a routine debt-evader retrieval mission, the two must survive waves of hostile contractors in a deadly homage to The Shining — complete with malfunctioning lifts, cryptic room keys, and an aggressively haunted ski instructor.

Corporate History

Mukluk’s original name was dropped after market testing revealed "Snow Boots" evoked cold feet and refund requests. Since rebranding, the Nothing Corporation has leaned heavily into curated exclusivity: the entire planet is technically an invitation-only wellness experience. Most guests never leave. (Or if they do, they’re quiet about it.)
