Biography

Butterfly is an ethereal lepidopteran alien known for their mastery of illusions and diplomacy. Using their natural charisma and perceptive abilities, Butterfly excels in resolving conflicts and creating diversions. Their enigmatic nature often leaves both allies and foes wondering about their true intentions.

Key Moments

  • Used illusions to infiltrate a heavily guarded Technobandit facility
  • Negotiated a critical trade deal between the Moonshine Crew and the Dispatch
  • Created a diversion to rescue hostages during a Skull Pirate raid

Role in the Universe

Butterfly's ability to manipulate perception and navigate complex negotiations has made them an influential figure in the Freelancers Guild. While their true motivations remain unclear, their actions have had a significant impact on intergalactic politics.
