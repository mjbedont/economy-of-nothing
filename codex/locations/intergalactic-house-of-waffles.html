Intergalactic House of Waffles
Codex Home Places Humans Aliens Robots Creatures

Overview

The Intergalactic House of Waffles (IHOW) is a galaxy-spanning diner chain and occasional metaphysical anomaly. Often appearing when least expected—and never where legally zoned—the House of Waffles is a frequent meeting spot for mercenaries, dispatch crews, corporate officers, cultists, and confused time travelers.

Known Features

  • Infinite menu (only two items ever available)
  • Bottomless coffee. Literally. A black hole at the base of every pot.
  • Bathrooms lead to different dimensions depending on your cholesterol level
  • All locations staffed by clones of a server named “Judy,” who may or may not be a hive mind
  • Signature dish: "The Existential Stack" – waffles served with a side of doubt

Appearances

  • Episode 004: Featured as the proposed neutral meeting ground during the birthday chaos and crew debate
  • Rumored to appear in at least three future dream sequences and one legal deposition

Codex Connections

  • Captain Tracy – Insists on ordering pancakes every time. Has never successfully received them.
  • Mr. Green – Secretly co-owns a location on Planet Prime under a pseudonym.
  • Cheyenne – Refuses to eat here. Claims it’s a distraction from the mission. She’s not wrong.
