Biography

Ryan McDonnell is a co-host of The Economy of Nothing Podcast. While often mistaken for Cheyenne in-universe, Ryan is very much his own character—frequently veering conversations into existential territory and always pushing the plot one layer deeper than it needed to go. He was the first to demand *The Prince of Egypt* be canonized into the lore of the show.

Ryan is often the driving force behind unexpected plot twists, story recursion, and music-based narrative detours.
