The Pooh Crew

"The Bears of Dispatch"

Summary

  • Type: Dispatch Crew
  • Species: Bears
  • Known Associate: Lance
  • Base: Dispatch, Planet Prime

Overview

Very little is known about the Pooh Crew, aside from the fact that they are a fully operational team of four bears who respond to dispatch jobs. How they communicate or qualify remains unclear. They are accompanied by Lance, who may or may not "talk to the bears."

Role in the Universe

The Pooh Crew exists in a liminal space between mystery and legend, often completing missions nobody else wants. The fewer questions you ask, the better.
