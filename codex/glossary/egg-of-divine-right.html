Egg of Divine Right

🥚 Royal Artifact. Possibly Sentient. Definitely Missing.

Codex Home 📍 Dracronaria 👩‍💼 Cheyenne 🧢 Captain Tracy 🛸 Mr. Green

Overview

Once believed to be a sacred relic of the Dracronarian monarchy, the Egg of Divine Right served as both a literal and symbolic source of royal legitimacy. Its magic? Unverifiable. Its origins? Disputed. Its current location? Probably somewhere under Mr. Green’s couch.

Notable Properties

  • Color: Gold-ish. Or pearlescent. Or holographic, depending on lighting and who’s asking.
  • Function: Confers legitimacy, inspires devotion, occasionally hums threateningly.
  • Rumored Powers: Hatches dragons. Or chickens. May poop gold.

In the Field

The Egg was reported stolen from a locked throne room, setting off a bureaucratic spiral of magical paperwork, surveillance orb rewinds, and questionable field decisions. Cheyenne investigated. The Captain doubted. Mr. Green wandered off and hit the jackpot.
