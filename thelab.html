Experimental Module: Pay to Lose™

The official training simulator of the Nothing Corporation. Navigate a board game where progress is punishment and the objective is to break even. Accumulate debt. Avoid success. Try not to get eliminated.

How to Play:

  • 🎲 Click “ROLL” to move forward
  • 📉 Random events will affect your money and debt
  • 💥 Game ends when your balance hits zero
  • 📊 A leaderboard tracks your session scores

May induce fiscal paranoia and/or spiritual liquidity events.
