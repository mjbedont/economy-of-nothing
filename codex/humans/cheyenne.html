Biography

Known throughout the Dispatch ranks as Captain Cheyenne, her true name—Shyan—originates from a mysterious orphanage she rarely discusses. This early life in the shadows shaped her leadership style: precise, quiet, and powerful.

Cheyenne leads the Gold Tier Crew, a matryoshka-style fleet operated by a hand-picked force of unpaid interns. She balances her dominance in the Dispatch system with compassion for those under her command, though few know what drives her beyond leaderboard perfection.

Key Moments

  • Deployed the Sunshine — a nesting doll-style ship — staffed entirely by interns
  • Oversaw a mission to retrieve the Dragon Egg on Dracronaria
  • Outranked Captain Tracy on the Dispatch leaderboard, sparking tension
  • Rumored to be secretly funding an orphanage or training facility for lost youth
  • Operated as both a captain and a pilot, qualifying her for multiple Dispatch categories

Role in the Universe

Cheyenne is one of the highest-ranking officers in the Dispatch system. Her ability to manipulate both strategy and image has made her a force in galactic operations, and a rival or ally depending on the mission. Though technically outranking Captain Tracy, their philosophical split is what defines their dynamic: he wants to do good. She wants to do better.

Whether Cheyenne is building a legacy, a rebellion, or a rescue program — only she knows. And she’s not telling.
