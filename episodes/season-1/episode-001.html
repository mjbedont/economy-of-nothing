Cowboys vs. Pirates: A New Idea

Dispatch 001: Captain Tracy takes the Moonshine crew to the dusty corporate wasteland of Brenner 7 for their first real job. But what starts as a simple freight run turns into a shootout with space pirates, a standoff with a robot sheriff, and a very complicated invoice.

This episode sets the tone: weird planets, broken systems, and a captain who’s doing his best even when his best looks reckless. Oh, and the pirates? They might not be the real villains here.

🧾 Notable Moments

  • 🚀 Tracy lands on Brenner 7 (after two failed attempts)
  • 🤖 Introduction of the Sheriff-Bot (Model BR-77)
  • 💥 First Moonshine vs. Skull Pirates shootout
  • 📦 Missing cargo turns out to be nothing… literally
  • 📡 Mr. Green says: “Trust the paperwork, not the people.”

📚 Lore References

  • Brenner 7 – Cowboy planet run by logistics firms
  • Skull Pirates – Our spacefaring antagonists
  • Sheriff-Bot BR-77 – The only law that runs on lithium
