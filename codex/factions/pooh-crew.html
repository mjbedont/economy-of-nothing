The Pooh Crew

Summary

  • Base: Unknown (believed to operate out of a retrofitted woodland habitat aboard Dispatch)
  • Handler: Lance
  • Affiliation: Dispatch (unofficially)
  • Known Members: Four bears, only referred to by codenames: Growler, Tumble, Blinky, and Clawpaw
  • Status: Operational, barely

Overview

The Pooh Crew is one of the most inexplicable units operating under the Dispatch umbrella. Composed entirely of bears with enhanced cognition, rudimentary speech, and questionable obedience, they are led (or more accurately, wrangled) by a single human handler named Lance. No one is sure why they exist. No one can figure out how they’re still funded.

Despite their oddball nature, the Pooh Crew has completed high-risk salvage, recon, and even diplomatic missions—though the outcomes are rarely clean and never repeatable. Some claim they were part of an early biogenetic taskforce. Others think they’re a prank that got out of hand. Lance insists “the bears are the only ones who still listen.”

Known Operations

  • Mission: Bear Hug Infiltrated a rogue amusement colony by pretending to be animatronics. It worked—until they stopped pretending.
  • Black Forest Recovery Retrieved a downed satellite using claws, intuition, and what can only be described as “aggressive cuddling.”
  • The Sandwich Incident No official records exist. All related footage is corrupted. The word “mayoquake” appears repeatedly in reports.

Reputation

The Pooh Crew is equal parts feared and adored. Their unpredictability makes them a last resort, but their sheer persistence gets results—eventually. They occupy a strange mythic tier among Dispatch crews, passed around in rumors, memes, and one horrifying children’s book adaptation.

Notable Quotes

  • "You can’t outgrow a bear. But you can outrun one... sometimes." — Lance
  • "Honey is non-compliant. We are not." — Blinky (allegedly)
  • "We eat the mission." — Graffiti found on their ship wall
  • "Bears go where logic won’t." — Dispatch threat response report
