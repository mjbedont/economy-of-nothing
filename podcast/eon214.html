The Skull Pirates are staking out the Nothing Corp dispatch. The Captain wants to finish his beer before clocking in. Nobody is going anywhere just yet.

Skull and Scoliosis run surveillance on Mr. Green and the Captain as they drop off a JJ Abrams mystery box — debating whether to steal it in transit or wait, what's actually inside it, and whether two skull-headed pirates is really enough crew. Meanwhile the Captain and Mr. Green sit in the truck, avoid the elevator song, and find some pills from Galaxy Seven.

From there: a Knight of the Seven Kingdoms review derails into a twenty-minute conversation about medieval filth, open sewers, the 1918 anti-spitting campaign, and how manners are just society's patch notes. Then Disney layoffs, severance math, the Kardashian talking-about-nothing industrial complex, South Park's Camp of Tolerance, and a long riff on why talking is easy but saying something is hard.

Parking Lot Depression. We're not going in yet.
