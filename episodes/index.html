The Economy of Nothing: Season 1

In a cosmos controlled by corporations and dictated by profit, Captain Tracy and his crew of the Moonshine are out to prove there’s another way to get by. Answering distress calls and doing the jobs nobody wants, they fight to stay true to their ideals while climbing the Dispatch leaderboards—one impossible mission at a time.

Episode List

1. Cowboys vs. Pirates: A New Idea

The Moonshine crew heads to Brenner 7, a corporate cowboy planet, to take their first dispatch job. They clash with space pirates and meet a mysterious robot sheriff, setting the stage for their ongoing struggle against a rigged system.

2. The Dragon Egg Heist

On the medieval planet Dracronaria, Tracy’s crew is hired to retrieve a stolen dragon egg. As they navigate court intrigue and rebel factions, they realize they’re in over their heads—and not everything is as it seems.

3. COVID-19-99

A trip to a 1999-themed planet takes a bizarre turn when Y2K isn’t a computer virus—it’s biological. The crew must contain the outbreak while navigating outdated tech and ‘90s pop culture references.

4. AI Run Amok

When a corrupted Tamagotchi Mr. Green salvaged from 1999 begins to wreak havoc at the Dispatch, the crew must survive an AI meltdown threatening to erase them all.

5. The Nothing Department

In a light-hearted office-comedy episode, Tracy’s crew is forced to deal with corporate red tape, HR disasters, and infuriating bureaucrats as they take on a seemingly simple task at Dispatch. Nothing is ever easy when the system itself is the enemy.

6. MoneyBall

Alex’s dream of seeing a live MoneyBall game comes true, but when the crew realizes the sport is rigged for profit and betting, Tracy must throw three “first” pitches while figuring out how to keep the team’s spirits high.

7. Mid-Dispatch Murder

A murder at the Dispatch sets Tracy’s crew in the middle of an office-wide investigation. With plenty of suspects, including their usual rivals, they must untangle the mess before their next mission.

8. Not a Mistake

A recall notice is placed on Erik, triggering a bureaucratic nightmare for the crew. As Tracy battles paperwork and red tape, Erik’s fear of being unwanted pushes him to a breaking point.

9. Sparkicuous

A failed dispatch mission lands Mr. Green in the New Romans’ gladiatorial pits. As he fights for survival, Tracy confronts the harsh reality of their place in the corporate food chain.

10. Plan A

Tracy reluctantly takes a corporate salvage job, throwing Mr. Green a bone after his ordeal. But things go horribly wrong, and Tracy’s worst fears are confirmed—the Nothing Corporation had been sabotaging them all along.

11. Die Hard on a Space Elevator

Mr. Green channels his inner action hero when the Rangers are taken hostage by skull-faced pirates aboard a space elevator. Meanwhile, Tracy and Captain Shyan hash out their differences below.

12. How Can the Future Suck, Now?

A ghost ship mystery turns into a gas-leak hallucination as the crew finds themselves trapped in a strange series of events, blending their present-day struggles with old-school space adventure tropes.

13. Captain Tracy and the Mystery of the Ghost Ship

The crew faces an eerie adventure that feels straight out of an old cartoon, but the truth is far stranger—and more dangerous.

14. Project Runaways

The fashion planet Malenberg hosts a competition with deadly stakes. Tracy’s crew finds themselves in the middle of a corporate conspiracy tied to the unethical production of anti-aging fashion products.

15. Return to Brenner 7

Back on the cowboy planet, the crew uncovers a deeper plot involving the robot workers, forcing them to make a stand against corporate oppression and old allies turned enemies.

16. Robin Hood Men in Suits

Tracy teams up with a group of cosmic thieves for a high-stakes heist, but even in a suit, he’s still got his principles. Can they steal from the rich without becoming just like them?

17. OMG, Y2K is Back Again

The Y2K virus has returned in the worst possible way—this time, it’s cosmic. The crew must fight to stop a new wave of digital chaos, but can they survive another ‘90s throwback?

18. Who Scoops the Scooper’s Poops?

On a planet of sentient dogs, Tracy’s crew faces an unexpected dilemma: the dogs’ virtual pets are being corrupted by a familiar rogue AI. Mississippi, Tracy’s old friend, is caught in the middle.

19. The Return of the Wyrm Keepers: A Reckoning of Dracronaria: The Fall of the Phoenix Queen

Back on Dracronaria, the crew faces a full-blown fantasy epic as Queen Temelairailailailailai rises as the Phoenix Queen. Tracy must navigate magic, rebellion, and space politics to restore balance.

20. High-Speed Corporate Chase

The crew is caught in a deadly race, their rivals in hot pursuit, as they try to escape a world that’s about to blow—literally. High-stakes action meets cosmic capitalism in a breakneck chase.

21. Die Hard in an Office Building

A final showdown begins as Tracy’s crew tries to take down the Nothing Corporation from the inside. But the board has its own plans, and nothing is ever as simple as it seems.

22. Never Forget

The Nothing Corporation is destroyed—or is it? Tracy’s victory is bittersweet as he realizes the system itself may be too big to fail. The crew’s next move? Back to square one, in a galaxy that’s always one step ahead.
