Dispatch

"The Nerve Center of Planet Prime"

Summary

  • Type: Intergalactic Logistics and Operations Center
  • Location: Planet Prime
  • Affiliated Crews: Moonshine Crew, Red Hooks, Shyan’s Crew, the Pooh Crew, Rough Ryders
  • First Appearance: Episode 1

Overview

Dispatch is the corporate operations hub on Planet Prime, coordinating assignments and logistics across the Nothing Corporation's outer territories. From low-level delivery runs to top-tier salvage operations, it's where every job gets assigned and every crew jockeys for front-page placement.

Role in the Universe

Dispatch is more than just a job board—it's the central arena where crews compete for reputation and rewards. It represents both the promise of opportunity and the bureaucracy of corporate control.
