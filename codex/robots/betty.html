Biography

B.E.T.T.Y., the Rough Ryders’ ace pilot, is an advanced tactical bot designed for high-pressure navigation and combat scenarios. Equipped with a cutting-edge AI, B.E.T.T.Y. excels in maneuvering starships through treacherous space environments and outwitting enemy fleets. Her sarcastic wit and quick decision-making make her a beloved member of the crew, as well as a formidable ally in the heat of battle.

Key Moments

  • Evaded a Technobandit blockade in Episode 4, ensuring the Rough Ryders' survival
  • Defeated a Skull Pirate fleet in a daring space battle during Episode 9
  • Collaborated with Captain Tracy to execute the Hyperspace Counterattack

Role in the Universe

B.E.T.T.Y. is the backbone of the Rough Ryders’ mobility and success in space battles. Her advanced AI and combat expertise allow the crew to undertake high-stakes missions with confidence. Beyond her technical capabilities, B.E.T.T.Y.’s personality brings a unique dynamic to the crew, blending humor and determination to inspire her allies and unsettle her enemies.
