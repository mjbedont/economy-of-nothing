Pay to Lose

A board game where the rich stay rich, the poor stay poor, and winning is the worst thing that can happen to you. Welcome to late-stage capitalism: the game night edition.

🎲 Monopoly Squid Game Planet: The crew imagines a galactic board game empire where chance decides your breakfast and debt is currency. Cheyenne tries to unionize the dice. Mr. Green weaponizes luck. Tracy builds a hotel and regrets it instantly.
