Overview

The Barrel Blaster 4000 isn’t just a sniper rifle. It’s a portable chain of bad decisions. Originally developed as a demolition tool by the Nothing Corporation's marketing arm (not R&D, oddly), the BB4K delivers high-velocity detonation pods with suspicious precision. It does not pass safety inspections. It does, however, pass through walls.

Features

  • Blast Radius Dial™: Adjustable settings from *Disruptive* to *Diplomatic Incident*
  • SmartScope: Includes predictive targeting, sarcasm filter, and self-esteem boost
  • Ammo Type: Emotionally volatile compressed charges (handle with therapy)

Field History

Mr. Green reportedly acquired the weapon during a “Weapons Expo” gone sideways. He claims it was “on sale” and has since used it in over a dozen assignments—most of which technically no longer exist. According to him: “Why shoot someone *near* an explosion when you can shoot the explosion *at* someone?”
