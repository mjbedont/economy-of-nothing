This week: a fuck-marry-kill gone wrong, a return-to-sender scheme, and a suspicious postman named Bobert.

Captain Ray Tracy and Mr. Green pick up right where they left the JJ Abrams mystery box: undelivered, on Burner 7, for reasons Green blames on dehydration and blackout-inducing space medicine. The fix? Mail the box back to the Nothing Corporation and let someone else deal with it. From there the episode detours hard — Fight Club, Spinoza, a full explainer on flash mobs, fireworks-induced PTSD in dogs and people, and a fully non-canon IHOW pitch meeting for a Mississippi-led spin-off called Puppy Posse.

Back in the story, Green tries to make the drop — and gets tangled up with Postman Bobert, who might just be the robot marshal in a fake mustache. The box's fate is, once again, to be continued.
