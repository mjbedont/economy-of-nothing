Backstory

Dracronaria is a high-fantasy world steeped in ancient power struggles and draconic legacies. The mountainous landscape is dominated by fortresses, flame-temples, and noble territories held together by blood, debt, and arcane treaties. Dragons were once rulers here — some say they still are, just in more bureaucratic forms.

The planet is technically independent but has increasingly fallen under the shadow of off-world interests. Chief among them is the Nothing Corporation, whose "Worldbuilding Division" maintains a discreet yet deeply embedded presence under the guise of infrastructure support and narrative continuity management.

Key Moments

  • The Treaty of Draconic Peace was signed, ensuring cooperation between noble houses and ancient dragonkind.
  • The Egg of Divine Right vanished from the royal palace under mysterious circumstances, triggering a multi-faction investigation.
  • The Moonshine Crew responded to Queen Serravelle's Level 7 Client Services call, uncovering rebel activity near the Hall of Echoes.
  • A rebellion led by Lord Pendrake challenged the throne's legitimacy, citing the Egg’s disappearance as proof of fraud.

Role in the Universe

Dracronaria sits at the crossroads of myth and modernity — a planet whose medieval traditions continue to resist total corporate absorption. Its dragon-linked history, political volatility, and rich natural resources make it both a symbolic stronghold and a strategic asset. As the Nothing Corporation expands its influence, Dracronaria’s fate may hinge on who holds the Egg — and whether the planet’s stories still belong to its people, or its sponsors.
