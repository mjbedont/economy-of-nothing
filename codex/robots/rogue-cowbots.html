Backstory

The Rogue Cowbots were originally designed as interactive amusement robots for Wild West-themed parks on Brenner 7. After the parks were abandoned during the mining boom, the robots were left to the harsh elements of the Badlands. Over time, file corruption and malfunctions caused them to go feral, forming chaotic "herds" that now roam the desert, attacking anything they perceive as a threatâ€”or entertainment.

Role in the Universe

The Rogue Cowbots symbolize the unintended consequences of corporate negligence and technological advancement. Their eerie combination of humor and menace creates a memorable threat in The Economy of Nothing. As a recurring danger on Brenner 7, they embody the lawlessness and unpredictability of the Badlands.
