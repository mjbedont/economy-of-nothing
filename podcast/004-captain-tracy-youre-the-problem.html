Episode 004 – Captain Tracy, You're the Problem

Episode Summary

Captain Tracy might be the savior of everything, but that doesn’t mean he’s the hero of our story. In this brutally honest birthday episode, the crew debates who gets to be the protagonist and why. Cheyenne's new Codex page goes under the microscope, Mr. Green’s motivations evolve, and Ryan starts a fight about the definition of the word “protagonist.”
