Choose Your Own Surveillance Orb

Three leads. One missing egg. Zero agreement. On the throne-world of Dranaria, a sacred relic is missing, and all signs point to magic. Captain Tracy, Mr. Green, and Cheyenne investigate — but each has a different plan, a different priority, and possibly a different reality.

✅ Agreed-On Things (For Now)

  • Cheyenne is a corporate agent running customer service for the Nothing Corporation’s worldbuilding division — not a mission-based freelancer.
  • The Nothing Corporation regularly sends multiple agents to the same site, often without telling them, to evaluate outcomes competitively.
  • Dranaria is a magic-heavy feudal world maintained for narrative integrity — meaning spells, monarchs, and ceremonial relics are considered "on-brand."
  • The Egg of Divine Right might be alive, autonomous, or enchanted — but nobody agrees on what it actually is.
  • Cheyenne, Mr. Green, and the Captain each pursue different investigation paths: protocol, profit, and vibes.
  • We’re canonizing choose-your-own-investigation formats and multiple realities for how stories might unfold.
  • Mr. Green's gear continues to evolve with plot demands. Laser pointer? Whip? Shield? Yes.
