Understanding Story Terms & Creative Impasses

Protagonist:

The main character whose decisions drive the story forward, undergoing significant transformation or growth.

Antagonist:

A character or force opposing the protagonist, creating narrative conflict.

Foil Character:

A character who contrasts the protagonist, emphasizing their traits through direct comparison.

Character Arc:

The emotional and psychological journey a character experiences through the story.

Theme:

The core idea or message explored in the narrative, such as "enough is never enough" or "certainty and its pitfalls."
