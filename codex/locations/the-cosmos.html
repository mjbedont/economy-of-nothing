The Cosmos: Divided by Corporate Interests A confederacy of corporate-controlled planets with federal oversight. Territories are gerrymandered to favor corporate interests, ensuring corporate dominance. The Nothing Corporation: Controls vast territories and key locations in the cosmos. Cosmic Services: Infrastructure and technology providers with pervasive influence. Cosmic Agriculture: Dominates food production territories. Cosmic Shipping and Logistics: Oversees transit routes and shipping hubs. Cosmic Entertainment: Controls media and luxury-focused planets. Cosmic Health: Specializes in healthcare and pharmaceuticals. Cosmic Finance: Manages financial systems and corporate deals. Coalition of Unified Neutral Territories: Neutral zones serving as buffers between corporate factions.
