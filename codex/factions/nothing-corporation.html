Nothing Corporation

  • Leader: Unknown CEO
  • Purpose: Maximize profits and minimize expenses
  • Key Activities: Exploiting resources, controlling the Dispatch
  • Notable Members: Captain Tracy, Moonshine Crew

The Nothing Corporation is one of the seven dominant corporations ruling the fractured cosmos of The Economy of Nothing. Known for its ruthless efficiency, it controls the Dispatch, contracting crews to perform high-risk missions for minimal rewards. Its ultimate goal is to perfect business, often at the expense of ethics or sustainability.
