Grasshopper

"The Agile Tactician"

Summary

  • Age: 29
  • Species: Alien (Amphibious Insectoid)
  • Role: Freelance Strategist and Recon Specialist
  • Personality: Quick-thinking, resourceful, highly adaptive
  • Affiliations: Freelancers Guild

Biography

Grasshopper is a highly skilled tactician and recon specialist known for their agility and sharp mind. An amphibious insectoid alien, Grasshopper thrives in both aquatic and terrestrial environments, making them a versatile asset in challenging missions. Their quick thinking and resourcefulness have earned them a reputation as a reliable freelancer.

Key Moments

  • Navigated treacherous terrain to deliver critical intelligence during the Battle of Brenner 7
  • Outwitted a Technobandit trap to recover stolen blueprints
  • Brokered a ceasefire between rival factions during a high-stakes negotiation

Role in the Universe

As a member of the Freelancers Guild, Grasshopper operates independently but is known for collaborating with various factions when their expertise is needed. Their agility and adaptability make them a valuable ally and a formidable opponent.
