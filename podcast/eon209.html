Alexa refused to play DMX.
Then things got weird.

A simple music request turns into a conversation about AI assistants, algorithmic nudges, and whether Big Tech quietly decides what we hear, watch, and buy.

This week we talk vertical monopolies, antitrust failures, data sharing between tech platforms, and why the modern economy feels increasingly coordinated.

Along the way we wander into crypto speculation, stablecoins, and the strange possibility that the entire financial system now runs on monetary relativism.

When value is relative,
whoever has the most money wins.
