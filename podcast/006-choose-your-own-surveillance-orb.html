Choose Your Own Surveillance Orb

When the sacred Egg of Divine Right vanishes from a locked throne room on Dracronaria, it's up to the crew—and Cheyenne—to untangle the magical mess. There's an arcane lock, a trail of legacy spellcraft, and a whole lot of arguing over protocol. Mr. Green, meanwhile, is just trying to figure out how his laser pointer became a whip.

🪄 Protocol vs Instinct: Cheyenne checks the mirrors. The Captain checks his gut. Mr. Green checks out entirely.
