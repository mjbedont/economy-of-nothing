Overview

The Wizard Bureaucracy is the interdimensional system of magical governance responsible for regulating spell usage, issuing wands, and enforcing documentation compliance. It is a labyrinth of scrolls, seals, and Rune C.

Key Rules

  • All spells must be declared prior to casting.
  • Unregulated enchantments require pre-clearance, form XR-7.
  • Anything involving dragons goes straight to Committee 9-B.
