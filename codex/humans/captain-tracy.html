Captain Tracy

Summary

  • Age: 36 (Standard)
  • Role: Captain of the Moonshine
  • Personality: Charismatic, stubborn, emotionally honest
  • Affiliations: Moonshine Crew, Nothing Corporation
  • Episode Appearances: All episodes

Biography

Captain Tracy once saved the universe. Then nothing changed. Now, he captains the Moonshine, taking dispatch gigs from the Nothing Corporation while quietly trying to outwork the system. Revered and forgotten in equal measure, he’s become a relic of a previous era — trying to teach the next generation that doing things the right way still matters, even when no one is watching. Beneath his flair and theatrics is a lonely man desperate to prove that decency isn't dead weight.

Key Moments

  • Saved the universe (offscreen) No one remembers how. No one seems to care. But the plaque on Brenner 7’s bar wall used to say “Savior of Everything.”
  • Adopted his nephews as Junior Rangers Turned family into crew. Believes teaching them by doing is better than keeping them safe and ignorant.
  • Publicly clashed with Dispatch protocol Refused to cut corners on a delivery mission, despite losing leaderboard points. Called it “a stupid system for stupid people.”

Role in the Universe

Tracy is the moral compass no one asked for. Unlike Cheyenne, who climbs the ranks, or Mr. Green, who games the numbers, Tracy believes in doing the right thing — even if it costs everything. He may be a relic, but he’s also a rebel: trying to teach the system to care, one reckless dispatch at a time.
