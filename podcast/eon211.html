And Walmart just patented your grocery bill.

They pivot through oil, crony capitalism, Mount Rushmore's dirtiest secret, the MLK memorial quote that got ground off the stone, Iran's nuclear breakout math, and sixty-two kids in Zimbabwe who saw something in 1994 that nobody wants to talk about.
