Pawdora

Overview

Pawdora is a planet entirely populated by sentient canines — not uplifted Earth dogs like Mississippi, but a distinct evolutionary lineage of dog-like beings who formed civilizations long before humans figured out indoor plumbing. Their society is advanced, hierarchical, and runs almost entirely on vibes and scented declarations.

Cultural Traits

  • Governance: A rotating council of pack alphas, guided by prophetic tail wags
  • Currency: Bones. Literal ones. No one's sure why it works, but it does
  • Core Values: Loyalty, territory, and dramatically timed howls
  • Forbidden Acts: Fake throws, vacuum worship, off-leash taxation

In the Show

The crew visits Pawdora during the events of "Who Scoops the Scoopers’ Poops", a diplomatic episode-turned-misadventure where Mr. Green is accidentally worshipped as a divine emissary. The arc begins subtly with a bone-sharing ceremony and escalates until he's at the center of a cult movement involving glowing tennis balls and mysterious belly-rub rites.

Mississippi vs Pawdora

Although Mississippi is a dog, she is not of Pawdoran origin. She was an Earth-born hound who gained sentience under unexplained circumstances (possibly related to a minor eldritch clerical error). The Pawdorans view her as something between a folk hero, a cautionary tale, and a really weird cousin who insists she’s "from the old country."
