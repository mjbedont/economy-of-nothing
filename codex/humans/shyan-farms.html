Biography

Shyan Farms is a highly respected and formidable captain in the Red Hook Crew, known for her strength, strategic mind, and fierce loyalty. Hailing from the chaotic underbelly of intergalactic space, she has built her crew from the ground up, always prioritizing the safety and wellbeing of those who follow her. Her leadership style blends strength with compassion, earning her respect from both her allies and adversaries.

Shyan has faced numerous challenges, but her steadfast dedication to her crew and her ability to navigate difficult situations has made her an invaluable ally to Captain Tracy and the Moonshine Crew. She has learned to balance her loyalty to her crew with the complex realities of intergalactic politics, knowing when to stand her ground and when to negotiate for the greater good.
