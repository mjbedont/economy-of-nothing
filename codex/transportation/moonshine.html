Backstory

The Moonshine started its life as an ordinary red pickup truck, rusting away in the corner of an old mechanic's shop on Planet Prime. Captain Raymond B. Tracy, in need of a reliable vehicle for short-distance hauls, purchased it for next to nothing. Little did he know, this humble truck would eventually become the backbone of his interstellar adventures.

After a series of unexpected events involving hyperspace anomalies and a hasty retrofit, the Moonshine was reborn as a spaceship. Its iconic red paint job and truck-like cockpit remained intact, making it a symbol of both nostalgia and ingenuity. Upgraded with state-of-the-art thrusters, reinforced plating, and glowing panels, the Moonshine now soars through space, blending rugged charm with high-tech functionality.

When Captain Tracy expanded his crew, the truck-turned-spaceship was modified again to include a crew cab, ensuring there was room for the Brettson Rangers and other adventurers. Despite its transformation, the Moonshine never lost its heart—a pickup truck with a story as big as the stars.

Specifications

  • Origin: Retro Pickup Truck from Planet Prime
  • Crew Capacity: 4-5 (Crew Cab Model)
  • Propulsion: Dual Plasma Thrusters with Hyperspace Boosters
  • Defenses: Energy Shields, Rear Laser Turret
  • Unique Features: Classic red paint, functional cargo bed, integrated hyperspace transmitter

Role in the Series

The Moonshine is more than a spaceship—it’s home. From daring heists to interstellar rescues, this quirky vessel symbolizes resilience and the unexpected ways ordinary objects can rise to extraordinary challenges. Its transformation from a humble Earth pickup truck to a key player in the intergalactic universe serves as a testament to Captain Tracy’s ingenuity and ability to make the best out of any situation.
