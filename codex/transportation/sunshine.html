Backstory

The Sunshine is a top-tier dispatch ship designed for maximum adaptability, command visibility, and nested fleet operations. Developed in part by Captain Cheyenne and assembled under Dispatch oversight, the ship features a layered, matryoshka-doll architecture—each internal vessel a fully deployable module, piloted by interns or junior rangers under her command.

Unlike traditional vessels, the Sunshine houses smaller tactical units within its frame, each capable of independent operation. The ship itself is a symbol of Dispatch elite status and one of the most advanced craft in known corporate space. Its gold-plated exterior is as much a marketing statement as a defensive shell.

Specifications

  • Structure: Tiered Matryoshka Configuration (Outer Shell + Intern Ships)
  • Crew Capacity: 1 primary captain + up to 24 intern-operated modules
  • Defenses: Reflective Armor Plating, Laser Eyes, Intern-Launched Microturrets
  • Propulsion: Golden Burst Drive w/ Fleet-Sync Control
  • Unique Features: Holographic Mission Briefing Dome, Intern Auto-Recall System, Painted Laser Eyes™

Role in the Series

The Sunshine stands as both a rival to The Moonshine and a reflection of Cheyenne’s perfectionist leadership style. Where Tracy’s ship is improvised and nostalgic, the Sunshine is tactical, hierarchical, and corporately optimized. Yet beneath the polished gold plating lies a network of overworked interns, balancing ambition and exhaustion at the edge of galactic burnout.

Its layered structure has become iconic among Dispatch captains—some call it genius, others call it excessive. Regardless, the Sunshine remains a symbol of Dispatch’s elite tier... and of the weight that comes with flying too close to perfection.
