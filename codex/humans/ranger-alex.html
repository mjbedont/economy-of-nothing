Biography

Ranger Alex Brettson is the 13-year-old nephew of Captain Raymond B. Tracy, and one of the youngest members of the Moonshine Crew. A passionate junior space ranger, Alex is enthusiastic, idealistic, and eager to prove himself. Despite his impulsive nature, he is a highly capable and dedicated member of the crew, contributing his skills in challenging situations.

Though Alex sometimes struggles with his desire for approval, he demonstrates great bravery and a sense of duty, making him an invaluable part of the team. His relationship with his uncle, Captain Tracy, is one of deep respect and admiration, and he often looks to him for guidance while simultaneously trying to carve out his own path.

Key Moments

  • Joined the Moonshine Crew on the mission to rescue hostages from the Skull Pirates
  • Assisted in the Battle of Brenner 7, showing bravery beyond his years
  • Helped broker peace between the Technobandits and Skull Pirates
  • Contributed to uncovering the Hyperspace Paradox, reshaping the course of history

Role in the Universe

As a junior space ranger and Captain Tracy’s nephew, Alex is a key member of the Moonshine Crew, offering fresh perspective and youthful energy. Although still learning the ropes, Alex’s willingness to take risks and his loyalty to his family and crew make him a promising young leader. His future in the crew is bright, with much potential to grow into an influential figure in the universe.
