Legacy Spellcraft

🧪 Outdated. Outlawed. Still oddly effective.

Codex Home 📍 Dracronaria 📚 Wizard Bureaucracy

Overview

Legacy Spellcraft refers to pre-corporate magical systems developed before the Bureau of Arcanic Oversight. These spells run on wild intention, unreliable runes, and emotional volatility—not approved channels.

Examples

  • Warding by Blood Memory — Effective, but sticky.
  • Summon Bureaucratic Delay — Banned due to infinite recursion.
  • Conjure Purpose (vague) — Results vary wildly.

Status

Technically illegal on most regulated worlds, especially those under corporate contract. Still widely used in the field by rebels, retirees, and rogue wizards who refuse to update their spellbooks.
