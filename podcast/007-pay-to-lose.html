Episode 007 – Pay to Lose

This is how the game gets made.

In this episode, Mike Allen, Ryan, and Mike Bedont improvise the foundation of what might be the most playable metaphor in the galaxy: a board game where the goal isn’t to win — it’s to survive with nothing left.

You'll hear the real-time evolution of The Economy of Nothing™: The Board Game, Cheyenne’s emotional arc taking shape, and the crew figuring out how macroeconomics, personal betrayal, and late-stage capitalism all fit into a game night from hell.

Plus: character emotions, Inside Out references, and the debut of the official FTS (*Free Thinker Society*) fan club.
